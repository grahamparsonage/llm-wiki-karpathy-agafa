Research paper proposing AGAFA, a neuro-symbolic framework for explainable digital forensics using MM-LLMs, ontologies, and ASP validation.

## Citation
Parsonage, G., & Dempsey, J. (2025). *Automated Generative AI-Driven Forensic Analysis (AGAFA). An Explainable Neuro-Symbolic Approach to Digital Forensics.* SKIMA 2025. IEEE.

## Key points
- Motivates DF backlog and timeliness pressures.
- Proposes a three-part pipeline: Neuro, Symbolic, Neuro-Symbolic.
- Uses ontologies for domain constraints and RAG/KAG support.
- Uses ASP rules to validate model inferences.
- Emphasizes legal admissibility, audit trails, and explainability.
- Includes a high-level sensitive-domain case example.
