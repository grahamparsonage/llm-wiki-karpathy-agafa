UK-focused high-level guidance for handling digital evidence across planning, capture, analysis, and presentation, anchored by the ACPO four principles.

## Citation
Association of Chief Police Officers (ACPO). (2011). *Good Practice Guide for Digital Evidence* (Version 5).

## Key points
- Establishes four foundational principles for digital evidence handling.
- Frames triage as a process, not a single tool.
- Emphasizes proportionality in seizure and forensic strategy decisions.
- Requires audit trails and reproducibility for third-party review.
- Reinforces competence requirements for accessing original data.
- Highlights reporting discipline, including clear fact vs opinion separation.
- Uses UK legal/procedural framing and disclosure expectations.

## Scope note
This source is UK-focused; jurisdictional transfer to other regions requires explicit adaptation.
