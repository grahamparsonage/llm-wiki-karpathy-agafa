## Citation
Kadage, A. D., Nayak, B. M., Hingmire, V. S., Wanjale, K., Bogiri, N., & Mandale, P. L. (2024). *AI-enhanced digital forensics: Automated techniques for efficient investigation and evidence collection*. Journal of Electrical Systems, 20(1s), 211-229.

## Key points
- Describes AI-assisted automation patterns intended to reduce manual bottlenecks in DF evidence collection and preliminary analysis.
- Outlines benefits in throughput and case prioritisation while noting trust and validation requirements.
- Highlights practical concerns around model transparency, false positives, and admissibility-sensitive reporting.
- Aligns with existing wiki direction toward governed, auditable, human-supervised AI support.
